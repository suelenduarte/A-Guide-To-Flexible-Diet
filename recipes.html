Meatballs with Mashed Potatoes and Gravy From Ikea

Ingredients

  • Whole package of Meatballs
  • Gravy Savoury Sauce Mix
  • Mashed Potatoes Package

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 425 °F
  • Place the meatballs in an ovenproof dish for about 15 minutes
  • Place the contents of the mashed potato bag in a saucepan
  • Add a little bit of water or milk
  • Heat at medium heat for about 9 minutes while stirring. Mashed Potatoes should be ready then
  • Pour 300 ML of cold water into a saucepan and bring to a boil
  • Add the sauce gravy mix. Stir well

Corn Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 (15 ounce) can of sweet corn drained (not cream style)
  • 1 (15 ounce) can of sweet corn not drained (cream style)
  • 1 (8 ounce) package of sour cream
  • 1 box of jiffy corn muffin mix
  • 1 stick of melted butter

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350 ° F
  • In a large bowl mix all ingredients together
  • Pour into 8x8 or 9x9 inch pan
  • Bake in a pre-heated oven for 50 minutes

Salmon with SAS Citron and Dill sauce Ikea

Ingredients

  • 4 fillets of Salmon
  • 1 Packet of SAS Citron and Dill sauce

Instructions

  • Preheat oven to 350 °F
  • Place the fish in an oven-proof container
  • Gently shake the pouch for 10 seconds
  • Pour the sauce on top of the fish
  • Bake in a pre-heated oven for 20 to 25 minutes
